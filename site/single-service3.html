How we work

DISCOVERY CALL

A complimentary 15 minute Discovery Call with one of our designers so we can learn more about you and your project goals.

MEET / PLAN

We’ll meet with you in your home or schedule a follow up call per your convenience for about an hour to get to know you, your family and discuss your project needs in detail. Afterwards, you will receive a detailed proposal with our estimated cost and duration to complete your project.

SIGN

If you like the estimate and want to work with us (which we hope you do), sign the contract, provide a deposit, and we will begin work on your project. If you decide to go another direction, we will be sad, but won’t hold you to anything.

PROJECT BUILDING

It’s time to start your project. Our team of architects, interior designers, vendors, subcontractors and construction managers\ will work with you throughout the design and construction process to come up with an awesome design that fits your needs, tastes and budget.
